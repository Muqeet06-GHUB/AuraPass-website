01
On-Device Inference
The behavioral model trains locally and runs entirely on your device. Every computation — sampling, scoring, baseline comparison — happens in your browser's JavaScript engine. No signals are transmitted. No server ever sees your patterns. The math happens where you are, and stays there.
02
Zero Network Egress
AuraPass makes zero network calls during authentication. Your behavioral fingerprint is computed locally and compared locally. Nothing leaves. We don't receive telemetry. We don't receive anonymous usage data. We receive nothing about your behavioral patterns — because nothing is sent.
03
No Persistent Storage
Behavioral patterns are held in-memory during the session only. Nothing is written to disk, localStorage, or any persistent medium by default. Enable the "Erase model on logout" setting for full ephemeral operation — the model is wiped entirely when the session ends, requiring a fresh profiling window next login.
04
Not A Surveillance Tool
AuraPass answers exactly one question: is this the same person who set up this session? It does not build behavioral profiles for advertising. It does not sell data. It does not report your patterns to any third party. It protects you — not from you.
